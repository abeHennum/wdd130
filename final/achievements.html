Premier League Titles (formerly First Division):

Manchester City has won multiple Premier League titles since its inception. Notable title-winning seasons include 2011-12, 2013-14, 2017-18, and 2018-19.

FA Cup Titles:

The club has won the FA Cup multiple times, with victories in 1904, 1934, 1956, 1969, 2011, and 2019.

UEFA Champions League:

Manchester City has consistently competed in the UEFA Champions League and has reached the semi-finals and finals of the competition. In 2022 they won their first Champions League title.

UEFA Europa League (formerly UEFA Cup):

The club has also competed in the UEFA Europa League and has reached the semi-finals.

Record Points Total:

In the 2017-18 season, Manchester City achieved a record-breaking 100 points in the Premier League, under the management of Pep Guardiola.

Domestic Cup Records:

The club has set numerous records in domestic cup competitions, including winning the EFL Cup in consecutive seasons and setting high-scoring records.
